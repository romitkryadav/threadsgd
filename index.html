Frequently Asked Questions- FAQ

Typically, such an item will fail due to incorrect or malformed URL or the item is not publicly viewable. Please verify you are copying the entire URL from your browser and the URL is valid for non-logged in viewers.

When you download a video or image from Threads, it will only be able to reproduce the quality it was originally uploaded as, when the video's or image's resolution change due to compression on Threads, the download will match that same compression. So if the video/image was reduced in size via compression, the download will also reflect a lower quality.

Why can’t I download content from private accounts?

Content from private accounts cannot be downloaded via this software because it does not have access to private profiles or anything contained in them. This software relies on all content being publicly available.

Does this tool impact my Threads profile in any manner?

Your Threads account has no interaction with this tool. It's simply designed for processing publicly available URLs. Creating or changing anything related to a user's Threads account requires them to do so on their own through the Threads website and/or application.

Why isn’t my download button responding?

There are a few reasons why your download button is not responding. It may be restricted by your browser or you may have installed an ad blocker or script blocker extension. Disabling one of those or switching browsers usually helps solve the problem.

Why does the downloader work on one browser but not another?

Because browsers implement user scripts and privileges differently hence if the downloader did not identify your browser as working it may still be possible that another browser would successfully find the downloader's application and download it or install it properly in that case.

Why does the downloader take longer for some posts?

Some posts take longer to download because they contain larger files that require more time for processing. Your internet connection speed will also have an impact on how long it takes for the results to appear.
