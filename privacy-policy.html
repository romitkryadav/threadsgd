Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Welcome to ThreadsGrab. ThreadsGrab is a browser-based, no-cost solution for downloading videos, photos, and profile pictures from any and all publicly available Threads content. Everything will be kept as user-friendly as possible, while still ensuring that the overall experience is both speedy and secure.

Information We Collect

When you visit the ThreadsGrab website, we will automatically collect some basic technical information about you. The following types of information may be collected:

  • Your IP address (for security and analytical purposes)
  • Your approximate location (country/region)
  • The type of browser you are using and what type of device you are using to access our website

The above information is used to provide us with insights into the way customers are using ThreadsGrab and to allow us to make improvements in speed, design and performance.

Cookies

To operate correctly and enhance usability, ThreadsGrab relies on utilizing cookies. The purpose of using a cookie is to record preferences, monitor traffic, and enhance functionality. If you would like to turn off cookies, you can do so in your browser's settings at any time. Please note that certain areas of the website may not function correctly without cookies.
