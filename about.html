About ThreadsGrab

ThreadsGrab

Threadsgrab offers a simple, fast and reliable way for you to save videos, images or profile pictures from your Threads account in a matter of seconds. This is useful to many people who find interesting or fun posts on Threads but cannot save them to their own device because the social media site does not include a download function in their interface. Threadsgrab's ability to allow users to save media files from the public post sections of Threads accounts provide a solution to this issue. Using the downloader is as easy as using your browser. Simply copy the link of the post you want to download and paste it into the field on our web page. Our downloader will automatically detect the media file(s) associated with the post. After that you simply choose the file you want (either video or photo) and download it directly to your device without having to install any apps or sign up for an account.

Our Mission

The goal behind Threadsgrab's existence can be summarized with the following statement: We believe that saving publicly available media files from Threads should be fast, simple and generally available to everyone. Many online downloaders are not very user friendly (slow, advertisement filled or require other downloads). We created Threadsgrab to provide a user friendly experience – allowing users to download Threads content in less than 10 seconds. When using Threadsgrab you can expect speed, dependability, and ease of use – allowing everyone to download video or image files from Threads, regardless of technical expertise.

User Privacy

Your privacy is very important to us. Threadsgrab will not keep or have any videos, images, or personal information stored. The tool only temporarily processes the Threads link to create a downloadable file. When the process has been completed, no information is retained on our servers.
