Why can’t the downloader find the video from my Threads link?

The video is playing but not downloading. What should I do?

Some browsers open the video in a new tab first. From there, you can right-click the video and select “Save video as” or use the browser download option.

Why is the video download taking too long?

Large video files or slow internet connections can make the process slower. Waiting a few seconds or refreshing the page usually fixes the issue.

Can I download multiple videos from one Threads post?

If the post contains multiple videos, the downloader will try to detect each video separately so you can download them.

Why does the downloader say “unsupported link”?

This usually happens when the link is not a direct Threads post URL. Make sure you copy the link from the share option of the post and not from somewhere else.

Why does the video download without sound?

In most cases the sound is included. If the video plays without sound, it may be because the original Threads video was uploaded without audio.

Why does the downloader sometimes show multiple download buttons?

If the Threads post contains different video qualities or multiple media files, the downloader may show more than one download option.
